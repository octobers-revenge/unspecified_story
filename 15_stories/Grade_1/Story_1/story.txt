**Title: The Great Map Adventure**

---

**Page 1**  
Once, there was a pup.  
The pup had a map.  
The map was big and red.  
The pup wanted to go on an adventure.  

---

**Page 2**  
The pup said, "I will find a spot!"  
He looked at the map.  
He saw a tree on the map.  
"Is that my spot?" he asked.  

---

**Page 3**  
The pup ran to the tree.  
When he got there, he saw a little boy.  
The boy sat on the ground.  
"Hello!" said the pup. "Do you want to play?"  

---

They played and had fun.  
They used the map to find other spots.
