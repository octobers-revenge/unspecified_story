**Title: Pam and Her Pup**

---

**Page 1**  
Pam has a pup.  
The pup is fun.  
Pam likes her pup.  

---

**Page 2**  
Pam and her pup run.  
They run up the hill.  
They run down the hill.  

---

**Page 3**  
Pam and her pup peek.  
They peek at a nut.  
The nut is on the ground.  

---

**Page 4**  
Pam will pick up the nut.  
The pup will help her.  
"Good pup!" says Pam.  

---

**Page 5**  
Pam and her pup have fun.  
They find a big box.  
"What is in the box?"  
"It is a gift for us!"  

---

Character description:
Pam is a cheerful young girl with curly brown hair, wearing a bright yellow dress and red shoes, accompanied by her playful brown and white puppy with floppy ears and a wagging tail.
