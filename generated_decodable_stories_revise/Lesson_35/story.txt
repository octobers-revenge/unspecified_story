UFLI Lesson 35: short a review (incl. nasalized a)

Pam has a big red van.  
Her pal, Sam, is with her in the van.  
Pam and Sam have a plan.  
They will go to the sand at the beach.  
Sam sees a map in the van.  
The map has a big red flag on it.  
"Look at the map, Pam," says Sam.  
They get to the sand and find the flag.  
Pam and Sam sit and pat the sand.  
They are glad about the fun they had.
