**Aisha’s Big Game**

---

Aisha Patel loves sports.  
She plays many games.  
Her best friend is Sam.  
Sam also loves to run.  
They play in the sun.

---

One day, Aisha sees a team.  
The team is called the Red Mints.  
They have a big game today!  
Aisha wants to join them.  
But she feels a bit shy.

---

Aisha tells Sam her plan.  
“Can I play with the Red Mints?”  
Sam smiles and says, “Yes!  
You can do it, Aisha!”  
Aisha feels happy and brave.

---

The game starts with a bang!  
Aisha runs fast like the wind.  
She kicks the ball to Sam.  
Sam scores a big goal!  
The crowd cheers, “Go, Red Mints!”

---

At the end, Aisha feels proud.  
She helped her team win!  
Aisha hugs Sam and says,  
“Let’s play again tomorrow!”  
They smile and run home.  

---
