**Aisha Patel and the Match**  

Aisha Patel loves to run.  
She wants to win a big match.  
Her friends are Ram and Sim.  

Aisha, Ram, and Sim train hard.  
They run in the sun each day.  
They play games and have fun.  

One day, Aisha sees a big mat.  
It is bright and red and blue.  
Aisha thinks it will help them.  

Aisha brings the mat to the field.  
Ram and Sim sit on the mat.  
They rest and drink from their cups.  

At the match, Aisha feels strong.  
She runs fast and wins the game.  
Aisha, Ram, and Sim are the best!
