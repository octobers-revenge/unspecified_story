**Aisha Patel and the Great Game**

---

Aisha Patel loves to play.  
She plays with her friends.  
They are ready for a big game.  

---

Aisha has a friend named Raj.  
Raj is good at catch.  
He can throw the ball fast.  

---

Aisha has another friend, Gita.  
Gita runs like the wind.  
She helps Aisha score goals.  

---

The game starts and it is fun.  
Aisha runs and kicks the ball.  
But oh no! The ball is stuck in the grass.  

---

Aisha and her friends work as a team.  
They dig and pull the ball out.  
Now they can play again and win!
