Phonics Pattern: g /g/ (the 'g' sound as in go)

**Aisha Patel and the Great Game**

---

Page 1:  
Aisha Patel loves to play.  
She plays in the park each day.  
Aisha has a red ball.  
She wants to play a game.  

---

Page 2:  
Aisha sees her friend, Gita.  
“Come and play!” Aisha says.  
Gita is glad to join.  
They kick the ball and giggle.  

---

Page 3:  
Then, Gagan comes to play.  
He brings his green goal.  
“Let’s play soccer!” he shouts.  
Aisha, Gita, and Gagan agree.  

---

Page 4:  
They run and kick the ball.  
Aisha kicks the ball to Gita.  
Gita kicks it to Gagan.  
The game is fun and fast!  

---

Page 5:  
But oh no! The ball rolls away!  
It goes into a big bush.  
Aisha, Gita, and Gagan get the ball.  
They cheer and go back to play!  

---  

The end!
