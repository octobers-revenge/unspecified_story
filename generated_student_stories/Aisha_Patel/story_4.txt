**Aisha Patel and the Big Game**  

---  

Aisha Patel is a girl who loves to play.  
She plays with her friends in the hot sun.  
Today is the big game at the park.  

---  

Aisha sees her friends Bob and Dot.  
They have a red ball and a big goal.  
Aisha wants to join and have fun.  

---  

Bob says, "Come on Aisha!  
We need one more to play."  
Aisha feels glad to be on the team.  

---  

The game starts and the ball is hot.  
Aisha runs and kicks the ball.  
She scores a goal and all shout "Hooray!"  

---  

Aisha feels proud and jumps with joy.  
Her friends cheer and clap for her.  
They all smile and say, "Let's play more!"
