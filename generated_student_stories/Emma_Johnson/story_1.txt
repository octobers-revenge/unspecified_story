**Emma Johnson and the Magical Fairytales**

---

Page 1:  
Emma Johnson loves to read.  
She likes to read fairytales.  
One day, she finds a book.  
It is a big book of fairytales.  

---

Page 2:  
Emma sees her friend, Sam.  
“Look at my book, Sam!” she says.  
Sam smiles and sits with Emma.  
“Let’s read about a magic man!”  

---

Page 3:  
In the book, there is a man.  
He has a magic hat and a map.  
The man helps a sad man.  
The sad man wants to be happy.  

---

Page 4:  
“Let’s help the sad man!” says Emma.  
“Let’s read more!” says Sam.  
They read and read and read.  
The magic man helps the sad man!  

---

Page 5:  
The sad man is now happy!  
Emma and Sam jump with glee.  
“Reading fairytales is so fun!”  
Emma smiles at her friend, Sam.
