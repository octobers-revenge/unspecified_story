Phonics Pattern: u /ŭ/ (short u sound as in cup)

**Emma Johnson and the Reading Fairytale**

---

Page 1:  
Emma Johnson loves to read.  
She likes to read fun books.  
One day, she finds a cup.  
The cup is big and blue.  
Emma sees a bug in the cup.  

---

Page 2:  
“Oh no!” says Emma.  
The bug is in the cup!  
Emma wants to help the bug.  
She looks for a way out.  
“Come on, little bug!” she says.  

---

Page 3:  
Emma has a big book.  
The book is about fairytales.  
She opens the book wide.  
“Let’s read a story!” she says.  
The bug jumps out of the cup.  

---

Page 4:  
The bug sits on the page.  
Emma reads about a fun prince.  
The prince loves a big, red truck.  
He goes to find his friend.  
“Let’s go on a trip!” they say.  

---

Page 5:  
Emma smiles at the bug.  
“Reading is so much fun!”  
The bug gives a little jump.  
Together, they read and play.  
Emma and the bug are happy!  

---
