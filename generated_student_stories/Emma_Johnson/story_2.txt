Phonics Pattern: t /t/ (the 't' sound as in tap)

**Emma Johnson and the Reading Fairytale**

---

Page 1:

Emma Johnson loves to read.  
She wants to read a fairytale.  
Emma has a big red book.  
It is fun to look at it.  

---

Page 2:

Emma sits on a soft mat.  
She sees her friend, Pat.  
“Hi, Pat! Come and sit!”  
Pat has a blue hat on.  

---

Page 3:

“Let’s read a fairytale!” says Emma.  
Pat says, “Yes! Let’s do that!”  
They open the big red book.  
It has a picture of a cat.  

---

Page 4:

“Oh no! The cat is sad,” says Pat.  
“Why is the cat sad?” asks Emma.  
“It lost its hat!” says Pat.  
Emma has a great idea!  

---

Page 5:

“Let’s help the cat!” says Emma.  
They search for the cat’s hat.  
They find it under a mat!  
The cat is now very happy!  

--- 

The end!
