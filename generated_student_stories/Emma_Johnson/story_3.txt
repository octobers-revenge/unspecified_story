**Emma Johnson and the Great Fairy Tale**

---

Page 1:  
Emma Johnson loves to read.  
She reads fairy tales every day.  
One day, she finds a big book.  
The book is full of magic!  

---

Page 2:  
Emma says, “I will read this!”  
In the book, she sees a frog.  
The frog is green and glad.  
“Hello, Emma!” says the frog.  

---

Page 3:  
“Can you help me?” asks the frog.  
“I lost my golden crown,” he says.  
“Help me find it, please!”  
Emma says, “Yes, I will help!”  

---

Page 4:  
Emma and the frog go to the gate.  
They see a big giant tree.  
“Is it under the tree?” asks Emma.  
“No, it is not!” says the frog.  

---

Page 5:  
They look by a small pond.  
The crown is in the grass!  
Emma picks it up with glee.  
“Here is your crown!” she says.  
“Thank you, Emma!” says the frog.
