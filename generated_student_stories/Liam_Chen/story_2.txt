Phonics Pattern: t /t/ (the 't' sound as in tap)

**Title: Liam Chen's Time Trip**

---

**Page 1**  
Liam Chen loves to tap.  
He has a big, red cap.  
One day, Liam finds a hat.  
It is not just a hat!  

---

**Page 2**  
The hat is a time hat.  
Liam puts on the hat.  
He feels a big zap!  
Liam starts to float!  

---

**Page 3**  
Liam lands on a tall tent.  
The tent is bright and neat.  
He sees a tall man.  
The man is named Tan.  

---

**Page 4**  
Tan says, “Welcome to the past!  
We have fun things to see.  
Can you help us, Liam?  
We need to fix the tent!”  

---

**Page 5**  
Liam and Tan work and tap.  
They fix the tent with a map.  
“Great job!” says Tan with a grin.  
Liam zaps back with a win!  

---  

Liam Chen loves to tap!
