**Liam Chen and the Great Green Gadget**

---

Page 1:  
Liam Chen is a brave boy.  
He loves to play with gadgets.  
One day, he finds a green gadget.  
"What is this?" he asks.  
It starts to glow and spin!

---

Page 2:  
Liam shows the gadget to his friend, Mei Ling.  
"Look at this great green gadget!" he says.  
Mei Ling giggles.  
"Can it go fast?" she asks.  
Liam smiles, "Let’s find out!"

---

Page 3:  
They press a big, red button.  
The gadget zooms up in the sky!  
"Wow! Look at it go!" Liam shouts.  
But wait, it starts to shake.  
"Oh no! What is wrong?" says Mei Ling.

---

Page 4:  
The gadget spins and goes in a loop.  
"It is going to crash!" yells Liam.  
Quickly, they think of a plan.  
"Let’s press the blue button!" says Mei Ling.  
They both press it, and it stops!

---

Page 5:  
The gadget lands safe and sound.  
Liam and Mei Ling cheer with glee.  
"That was great fun!" says Liam.  
"We saved the day with our gadget!"  
They both smile and play again.
