Phonics Pattern: u /ŭ/ (short u sound as in cup)

**Liam Chen and the Lost Ship**  

---  

Page 1:  
Liam Chen is a boy.  
He loves to read.  
He loves to read about space.  
One day, Liam finds a book.  
The book is about a lost ship.  

---  

Page 2:  
Liam sees a big picture.  
The ship is in a dark cup.  
“Wow!” says Liam.  
“I want to find the ship!”  
He takes the book to his friends.  

---  

Page 3:  
His friends are Sue and Jun.  
They like the book too.  
“Let’s find the lost ship!” says Sue.  
“Let’s go to the big park,” says Jun.  
They jump with joy!  

---  

Page 4:  
In the park, they look around.  
They see a big rock.  
“Is that the ship?” asks Liam.  
“No, it is just a rock,” says Sue.  
They keep looking for the ship.  

---  

Page 5:  
Then, they find a blue cup.  
Inside the cup is a clue!  
“It says to look up!” says Jun.  
They look up and see the ship!  
The lost ship is in the sky!  

---  

The end!
