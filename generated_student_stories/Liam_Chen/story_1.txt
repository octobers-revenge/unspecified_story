**Liam Chen and the Moon Mission**

---

Page 1:  
Liam Chen is a boy.  
He loves to dream.  
Liam dreams of the moon.  
He wants to go to the moon.  
“Mmm! That will be fun!” he says.

---

“Let’s zoom to the moon!”  

---

Page 4:

“Mmm! We are almost there!”  

---
