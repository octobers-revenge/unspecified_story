**Liam Chen and the Moon Men**

---

Liam Chen sat on his mat. He saw a light in the sky.

---

"Look at that light," said Liam. "What can it be?"

---

Liam ran to his friend Min. "Did you see the light?" he asked.

---

Min said, "Yes! It is the Moon Men!" They both jumped with glee.

---

The Moon Men came down to play. Liam and Min had fun with the Moon Men.
