UFLI Lesson 48: ch = /ch/

Chuck and his chum Chip sit on a bench.  
The sun is hot and high.  
Chuck has a hunch for lunch.  
He checks his lunch bag.  
It is full of yum fish and chips.  

Chip likes fish much.  
Chuck and Chip munch on lunch.  
Then a chick hops by and clucks.  
The chick pecks at a bunch of chips.  
Chuck and Chip grin at the sight.  

The chick pecks and pecks.  
It munches a bunch of chips.  
Chuck and Chip watch the chick.  
Lunch is fun with such friends.  
Chuck, Chip, and the chick sit in the sun.
