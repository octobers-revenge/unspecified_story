Phonics Lesson 37: short o review

Bop and his dog.
Mom has a box.
A fox and frog.

They spot the fox.
The dog can hop.
The fox is on logs.
Bop got a rod.

The rod got a cod.
Mom and Bop grin.
The frog went plop.
The dog will stop.
They pop back home.
