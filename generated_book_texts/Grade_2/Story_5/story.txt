Phonics Lesson 11: nasalized a (am, an)

**Title: Sam and Pam's Fun Day**

---

**Page 1**  
Sam is a boy.  
Pam is a girl.  
Sam and Pam are friends.  
They want to have fun.  

---

**Page 2**  
Sam has a fan.  
Pam has a tan.  
“Look at my fan!” says Sam.  
“Look at my tan!” says Pam.  

---

**Page 3**  
Sam and Pam go to the map.  
They look for a fun spot.  
“Let’s go to the big tree!” says Pam.  
“Good idea!” says Sam.  

---

**Page 4**  
At the tree, they sit.  
They eat a big snack.  
Pam has a fat jam.  
Sam has a tin dip.  

---

**Page 5**  
Sam and Pam have fun.  
They laugh and play.  
“Let’s do it again!” says Sam.  
“Yes! Let’s play all day!” says Pam.  

--- 

**The End**

Character description:
Sam is a cheerful boy with short brown hair, wearing a bright blue t-shirt and green shorts, and he carries a colorful fan; Pam is a joyful girl with long blonde hair, dressed in a sunny yellow dress, and she proudly shows off her tan.
